Chitkara University, Punjab has made secure and safe arrangement of transport facility for students, staff & faculty. The transport facility is available from Chandigarh, Mohali, Kharar, Patiala, Derabassi, Panchkula, Ambala, Kalka, Zirakpur, Landran, Rajpura, Banur, Khanna, Sirhind, Doraha and Ludhina. All the buses reach the campus by 8:45 am (Approximately) and leave the Campus by 4:45 pm (Approximately) or as per scheduled activities / events in the University.


GENERAL
  1. The transport facility, on payment basis, is arranged by the University to facilitate the Day Scholars. All the buses are centrally managed by the University Administration.
  2. Transport facility is for the full academic session and optional for the Day Scholars. Allotment is purely on first come first serve basis to the students who undertake to abide by the transport rules and regulations of the University.
  3. The University reserves the right to frame, amend, revoke, repeal and enforce the Rules & Regulations, as and when deem fit; and students shall be well versed and updated with the amended Rules & regulations as applicable from time to time. The University shall not be bound or responsible to intimate each student separately in any manner, in this regard.
  4. University has complete authority to inspect transport vehicle including personal belongings during transit or at any time in and outside the campus.
  5. The University reserves the rights to deny transport facility to any student on the grounds of misconduct/ Indiscipline.
  6. Ragging, by law, is strictly prohibited. Any student who is traveling in the university bus found indulging himself / herself directly / indirectly in any activities like theft / ragging / fighting / quarrelling / use of abusive language / misbehave with fellow students, juniors / seniors or Staff members, shall be considered a serious misconduct /indiscipline and strict disciplinary action shall be initiated against him / her as deemed necessary or may be handed over to police for legal proceedings according to nature of offence for which entire responsibility lies on the concerned student.
  7. Eve teasing and public display of affection are strictly prohibited in the University campus as well as during the transit. Any kind of violation will attract strict disciplinary action including expulsion from the University.
  8. Use of any kind of intoxicants (like Drugs, Smoking, Drinking) Gambling, Playing Cards, Photography (without permission) etc., are strictly prohibited in the University Campus as well as during transit. Students involved in any such act shall be liable for STRICT disciplinary action.
  9. The students availing the transport facility shall be responsible for all the personal belongings and the University management is not responsible for theft / loss of property during travel in bus.
  10. All students must reach at their respective bus stops at least 5 minutes before the scheduled departure time of the bus. Students will board the buses from the assigned boarding point/location only. They will board only that bus that has been allocated to them and occupy the allotted seat only.
  11. The Boarding is entirely at risk of the student availing transport facility. The University management does not own any type of responsibility towards compensation of any nature whatsoever.

  12. In case of breakdown of the University Bus due to mechanical fault or any reason, no charges towards alternative conveyance would be paid, however efforts shall be made to make alternate arrangement.
  13. No one would be compensated for the distance covered by student for boarding the bus from designated point.
  14. Buses will run on pre-determined routes as laid down by the University. Students will be assigned routes and pick up points. The same shall be subject to change from time to time.
  15. Students are advised to occupy their bus seat at least 5 minutes before the departure from University campus. No one will catch the bus while it is in motion.
  16. Any student found travelling in bus without having paid the transport fee, he/she shall be liable to be fined Rs.1000/- for first time and if repeated, student shall be assumed as a regular traveler and charged full transport semester fee plus fine.
  17. Students are advised to carry transport ID card (Bus Pass) all time during transit and are liable to produce the same as and when asked by the University authorities. Travelling without Bus Pass is strictly prohibited and for any violation a fine of Rs. 200/-will be imposed each time.
  18. Students are liable to pay for any damage caused by them to the transport vehicle or property therein either alone or jointly with others. In addition disciplinary action could also be initiated against them.
  19. Students staying in the hostels are not permitted to travel in the buses until and unless they have been specifically authorized (written consent from transport Department) to do so.
  20. No one plays loud music (Earphone music is permitted). No hooting, clapping, singing, loud talking in the bus is allowed.
  21. In case of violation of any rules & regulation or any instruction issued by the University Management, a penalty, in form of fine or cancellation of bus pass including expulsion from the University may be imposed on the student.
  22. Lost or stolen ID card can be re-issued on payment of Rs. 500/-.

ONLINE ALLOTMENT OF TRANSPORT FACILITY
  1. Transport facility allocation is purely online.
  2. Students will be able to choose the Route number from the available options only.
  3. Seat is allotted on the basis of availability of the seats in the bus.
  4. The allotment of seat is restricted to that particular session only.
  5. Before EVERY Session bus seat shall be allotted online to students subject to clearance of pending dues, if any.
  6. Bus facility can be withdrawn on disciplinary grounds.
  7. Students are not permitted to change Bus route on their own. Strict disciplinary action shall be initiated against defaulters.
  8. Student has to carry bus route allocation letter/fee receipt while coming to campus first time.

TRANSPORTATION RULES & REGULATIONS : CHITKARA UNIVERSITY (PUNJAB)

Bus Pass (Identity Card) will be issued to student on submission of Route Allocation Letter along with undertaking (Annexure - I) duly signed by parents/guardians in the transport department.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF TRANSPORTATION
  1. Validity of the bus pass is from July to June next year.
  2. In case the transport fee is deposited for one semester - it will be valid from July to December or January to June.
  3. In special cases, validity may be reviewed at the discretion of the University Management due to restructuring of course duration.
  4. It is mandatory for students to submit UNDERTAKING duly signed by parents / guardian, as given in the Annexure -1.
  5. Once the Transport Fee is deposited, student cannot change the stop / route without prior permission of the Transport Department.
  6. New bus pass with changed route and address will be issued upon consent from the parents/guardian along with payment of Rs 100/-.
  7. Bus pass cannot be transferred from one person to another. The bus pass owner must be the sole user of the pass. Transferring or sharing of a pass is prohibited. If such activity occurs, both participating parties’ transport privileges will be revoked. Competent Authority has right to cancel passes due to misuse.
  8. Bus Pass may not be reproduced or altered. The use of reproduced or altered pass is a violation of these regulations and will result in punitive action.
  9. Passes may not be sold, exchanged, given away, or purchased from any person or agency other than University Transport Department.

FEE PAYMENT
  1. Transport Fees is charged session wise i.e., on annual basis and the same shall be deposited in the month of June, every year.
  2. The students undergoing Industrial training can deposit one-semester fees with the prior approval of concerned Dean as well as transport department.
  3. The fee shall be paid by online mode only.
  4. Students are expected to pay full transport fee. Partial payments/Installments would not be entertained.
  5. Transport fee once paid will not be refunded /adjusted except for the student who leave the course during the first semester only.
  6. Fee is NON-REFUNDABLE if the transport is withdrawn on grounds of violation of rules & regulations.
  7. Transport charges are subjected to revision every year.

AMENDMENTS OF TRANSPORT RULES & REGULATIONS

The University reserves the right to modify / cancel or amend all or any of these rules and issues supplementary rules or amended rules without prior notice and shall be applicable with effect of notification of such amendment and the date on which the same is notified on the CHALKPAD.
